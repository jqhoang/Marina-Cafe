OUR STORY

We believe in our community and providing the highest quality food. Created with fresh produce from local farmers, our partners at Nappa Valley give us this oppurtunity.

Medhat Elmasry, Tommy Yeh, and John Hoang founded Cafe Marina in 1987. A blend of french-inspired cuisine with a basis in chinese and vietnamese dishes we deliver a unique flavour. Our coffee comes from bean cultivated from fair-trade partnered farmers in the tropical rainforests of Nigeria. Steamed to perfection we guarantee our lattes and cappucinos meet the standard , our baristas are professionally trained to create an experience in every cup.
